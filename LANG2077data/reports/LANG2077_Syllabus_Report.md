# LANG 2077 Course Report  
**Language Skills for Human-AI Partnership: Customizing Chatbots to Empower Communities**  
Hong Kong Baptist University – Language Centre  

---

## 1. Course Identity
- **Course Code:** LANG 2xxx (LANG 2077 pilot)
- **Credits:** 3 units
- **Medium of Instruction:** English
- **Delivery Mode:** Service-Learning intensive (May 23 – June 6, 2025) in Henan, China
- **Participants:** 31 HKBU students, 80+ local primary students, 9 themed teams

---

## 2. Aims & Objectives (Syllabus §7)
1. Develop students’ language skills within human-AI collaboration contexts, emphasizing communication for community empowerment.
2. Investigate AI-powered chatbots as solutions to local linguistic/educational challenges.
3. Examine interactions between human language and AI systems, and their social implications.
4. Apply linguistic expertise plus AI tooling to design community-responsive solutions.
5. Cultivate critical thinking and problem-solving to evaluate AI-driven interventions.

---

## 3. Course Content Structure (Syllabus §8)

### Part 1 – Foundations of Generative AI & Language
- Overview of large language models
- Language, culture, and technology intersections
- Critical evaluation of AI outputs (bias, limitations)

### Part 2 – Community Engagement & Solution Design
- Design thinking & needs assessment with community partners
- Communication strategies for chatbot dialogues, questioning, empathy
- Case studies:  
  - Rural STEM lesson generation bot  
  - Shenzhen mobile families’ school-admissions assistant

### Part 3 – Implementation & Evaluation
- Prompt engineering & conversational flow design
- Feedback loops for continuous improvement
- Impact assessment and storytelling/celebration strategies
