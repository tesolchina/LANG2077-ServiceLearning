# Educational Games Report - LANG 2077 Summer 2025
## Service-Learning Project: Interactive HTML/JavaScript Games for Rural Primary School Education

**Course:** LANG 2077 - Language Skills for Human-AI Partnership: Customizing Chatbots to Empower Communities  
**Institution:** Language Centre, Hong Kong Baptist University  
**Project Period:** May 23 - June 6, 2025  
**Location:** Rural Primary School, Henan Province, China  
**Target Audience:** 80+ primary school students (Grades 1-6)

---

## Executive Summary

This report documents 10 interactive educational games developed by 31 HKBU students organized into 9 specialized teams as part of the LANG 2077 service-learning project. These HTML/JavaScript games were designed to deliver AI literacy education and subject-specific learning to rural primary school students in Henan Province, China. Each game represents a collaborative effort between HKBU students and local educators, addressing specific educational needs identified through community needs assessment.

**Total Games:** 10  
**Total Student Developers:** 31  
**Total Teams:** 9  
**Educational Impact:** 80+ primary school students

## Educational Impact and Outcomes

### Learning Outcomes Achieved

1. **Subject-Specific Learning:** Each game targets specific curriculum areas, providing interactive learning experiences that complement traditional teaching methods.

2. **Technology Integration:** Students gained exposure to digital learning tools, enhancing their technological literacy and preparing them for modern educational environments.

3. **Engagement and Motivation:** Gamified learning increased student engagement and motivation, making learning more enjoyable and accessible.

4. **Skill Development:** Games developed various skills including problem-solving, critical thinking, language proficiency, and subject-specific knowledge.

### Community Impact

- **80+ Students Served:** Primary school students in rural Henan Province received interactive educational resources
- **Resource Creation:** Sustainable digital learning resources created for ongoing use
- **Teacher Support:** Games provide supplementary teaching materials for educators
- **Cultural Exchange:** Collaboration between HKBU students and local educators fostered cross-cultural understanding

### Alignment with Course Objectives

All games align with LANG 2077 course objectives:

- **CILO 1:** Games demonstrate effective communication design for diverse audiences (primary school students)
- **CILO 2:** Development process required critical evaluation of educational needs and game effectiveness
- **CILO 3:** Games address real community needs (educational resource gaps in rural schools)
- **CILO 4:** Technology integration (HTML/JavaScript) enhances communication and learning outcomes
