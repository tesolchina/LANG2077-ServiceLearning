## üìã **File Naming Conventions**

### **Photos:**
- Format: `YYYY-MM-DD_Activity_Description_StudentName.jpg`
- Example: `2025-01-15_Workshop_CommunityTraining_GroupA.jpg`

### **Documents:**
- Format: `YYYY-MM-DD_DocumentType_StudentName.pdf`
- Example: `2025-01-20_ReflectionReport_JohnSmith.pdf`

### **Presentations:**
- Format: `YYYY-MM-DD_PresentationType_GroupName.pptx`
- Example: `2025-01-25_FinalPresentation_TeamAlpha.pptx`

### **Deliverables:**
- Format: `DeliverableType_Version_Date.pdf`
- Example: `TrainingManual_v1.0_2025-01-15.pdf`
