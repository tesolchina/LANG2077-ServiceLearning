## üéØ **Project Overview**

### **Course Description**
Language Skills for Human-AI Partnership: Customizing Chatbots to Empower Communities

### **Service Learning Focus**
Students will participate in service-learning projects with community partners, where they will design, develop, and deploy customized chatbots to address real-world communication challenges.

### **Key Learning Outcomes**
- Develop advanced linguistic skills for human-AI interactions
- Apply critical thinking to evaluate AI system effectiveness
- Demonstrate cultural sensitivity in community collaboration
- Enhance communication strategies through technology integration
